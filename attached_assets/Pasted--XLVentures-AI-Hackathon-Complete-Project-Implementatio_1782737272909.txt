# Project Theme

SupplyPilot AI

An Agentic Decision Intelligence Platform for Supply Chain Operations.

The platform should determine the Next Best Action for supply chain managers using multiple specialized AI agents.

---

# Core Workflow

User selects or uploads a business scenario.

↓

Planner Agent analyses the request.

↓

Planner dynamically chooses specialized agents.

↓

Agents retrieve structured data and enterprise knowledge.

↓

Agents perform reasoning.

↓

Planner combines outputs.

↓

Recommendation Agent generates ranked Next Best Actions.

↓

Explainability Engine provides reasoning and confidence.

↓

Human approves or rejects.

↓

Memory stores outcome.

# Explainability Engine
