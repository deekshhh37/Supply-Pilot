# Vendor Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Standard Vendor SLA Terms

### Delivery Performance
- On-time delivery rate: ≥95% required for Tier 1 vendors
- On-time delivery rate: ≥88% required for Tier 2 vendors
- Any vendor below 80% OTD for 3 consecutive months is escalated to probation

### Lead Time Commitments by Vendor Tier
- **Tier 1 (Primary)**: 2-3 business days standard, 1 day expedited
- **Tier 2 (Secondary)**: 4-5 business days standard, 2 days expedited
- **Tier 3 (Emergency)**: Variable, typically 1-7 days

### Delay Notification Requirements
- Vendors must notify within 4 hours of discovering a delay
- Delays >24 hours require written explanation and revised delivery commitment
- Delays >72 hours trigger automatic escalation and alternate sourcing evaluation
- Vendors with 3+ unnotified delays per quarter face SLA penalty (2% contract value)

### Quality Standards
- Defect rate must be <0.5% for Tier 1 vendors
- Partial shipment accepted only with prior approval
- Substitutions require written approval from Procurement Manager

### Escalation Protocol
- Vendor breach → Procurement Agent notified immediately
- Breach >24h unresolved → Procurement Manager escalated
- Breach affecting Platinum/Gold customer SLA → Emergency Procurement activated
